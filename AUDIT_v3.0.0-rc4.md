# Bellwether v3.0.0-rc4 Audit — Multi-Playstyle Pacing & Strategic Intelligence

## Evidence-first audit scope
Inspected the RC3 authoritative player-identity derivation, Town Mind strategy state and daily pressure path, Story–Consciousness integration posture logic, resistance modifiers, systemic-horror integration, sleep/day-transition ordering, public developer-state exposure, and historical certification boundaries before editing.

## Confirmed findings
- Player identity already derived care, community, inquiry, routine, social, independence, resilience and avoidance.
- Town Mind already selected one of six pressure domains from current attachments and applied one bounded daily consequence from Day 2.
- Story–Consciousness integration already selected normalize/distract/isolate/contradict/confront postures from story progress, evidence, ordinary activity and avoidance.
- Resistance already reduced pressure through expiring protected domains.
- Genuine gaps: no explicit cross-lifestyle pacing classifier; no persistent adversarial hypothesis; no memory of resisted tactics; no tactic discounting after failure; no deliberate retreat; strategy history lacked hypothesis evidence.

## RC4 changes
- Added `playstyle_pacing_model.py`, classifying investigator, homesteader, entrepreneur, social and wanderer playstyles from authoritative state.
- Added pacing risk assessment (`balanced`, `stalled`, `rushing`) without altering authored chapter gates.
- Town Mind strategy schema upgraded to v3 with auditable hypothesis, confidence, tactic failures, outcome memory, retreat window and chain stage.
- Strategy scoring now considers playstyle while remaining deterministic and bounded.
- Repeatedly resisted tactics are remembered and discounted, forcing adaptation rather than endless pressure repetition.
- Two resisted applications can trigger a one-day deliberate retreat; retreat is recorded, not hidden.
- Developer status now exposes playstyle pacing overview alongside Town Consciousness overview.

## Authority boundaries
RC4 does not create evidence, alter story chapters, mutate ending eligibility, fabricate relationship canon, or let the LLM directly mutate authoritative state. Pacing classification observes; strategy adapts only through existing bounded pressure domains.
