BELLWETHER v1.0.1 — LIVING WORLD RUNTIME AUDIT
Date: 2026-07-09

PURPOSE
Activate the previously exposed but empty world_runtime surface as a persistent,
deterministic environmental substrate. Preserve v1.0 story, simulation, saves,
AI fallback, and postgame behaviour.

IMPLEMENTED
- Added backend/core/world_runtime_model.py.
- Persistent 30-day weather history.
- Slow tendencies for wet periods, drying pressure, pollinator activity, bird
  activity, soil saturation, and river pressure.
- Persistent location conditions for cottage soil, river level, and woodland ground.
- Player-visible environmental observations through Look Around.
- Weather-history consequences feed gardening growth and bounded economy delivery
  disruption signals without allowing the LLM to calculate authoritative state.
- Old saves migrate safely with runtime defaults.
- Developer simulation payload now exposes the real world runtime and ecology tendencies.

LLM EXECUTION-PATH AUDIT
The current intended AI execution paths were inspected before this release:
1. Weather Director: bounded weather-state choice in specific_directors.py.
2. NPC/traffic/ambient Director rounds: legal candidate selection in specific_directors.py,
   queued through the asynchronous runtime for ordinary background work.
3. Foreground conversation: ask_player_reply in game.py/provider.py; reply required before
   the player can continue, with deterministic fallback available.
4. Ambient conversation text: bounded ask_text path in specific_directors.py.
5. Town Mind: legal strategic-intention choice, asynchronous in normal runtime.
6. Procedural social arcs: legal template choice, asynchronous in normal runtime.
7. Initial season selection: bounded season choice with deterministic fallback.

AUTHORITY FINDING
No new LLM mutation authority was added in v1.0.1. The living-world runtime is
calculated by deterministic code from authoritative season, weather, time, and
existing state. This is deliberate for low-end hardware: strategic AI can later
influence bounded tendencies, while the deterministic runtime applies consequences.
