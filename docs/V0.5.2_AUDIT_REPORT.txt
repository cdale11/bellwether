BELLWETHER v0.5.2 AUDIT REPORT

Scope: Relationship Depth and Social Consequences.

Implemented:
- persistent structured social-consequence state;
- explicit promises, invitations, requests, apologies, insults, agreements and refusals;
- persistent grievances and apology resolution;
- open commitment and shared-history retrieval for core-NPC dialogue context;
- relationship-stage derivation from multidimensional state;
- auditable gossip log connected to catalogue-backed knowledge propagation;
- additive old-save migration.

Safety and consistency notes:
- Generated dialogue prose still cannot directly mutate objective state.
- Social-act extraction is deliberately conservative and requires explicit language.
- Ordinary conversation is not converted into commitments.
- Gossip remains restricted to the authoritative knowledge catalogue and existing trust/tension transfer rules.
- Romance is not forced or universally enabled in this release; the relationship substrate now supports later authored, character-specific romance eligibility.
- Promise fulfillment and deadline tracking are intentionally not inferred from arbitrary prose. Future quest/item integrations should resolve commitments through explicit engine events.

Validation performed:
- focused v0.5.2 diagnostic;
- v0.5.1, v0.5.0, v0.4.2, v0.4.1 and v0.4.0 regression diagnostics;
- cumulative post-v0.1.0 diagnostic suite;
- Python compilation;
- runtime state/view smoke test;
- JSON serialization and old-save migration checks;
- JavaScript syntax check where Node is available;
- archive integrity and executable permission checks.
