BELLWETHER v1.6.0 GEOGRAPHIC EXPANSION AUDIT

Scope: additive expansion over the accepted v1.5.0 baseline.

Implemented:
- 7 new locations and 3 regional districts, all validated by WorldModel.
- Bidirectional route connectivity into the existing river, quarry and village-road graph.
- New map anchors, reveal regions and route lines.
- Ordinary activities and hobby eligibility across expanded geography.
- Player status surface: health, hunger, energy, warmth, life XP, level and skills.
- Cottage condition continuity with climate deterioration and staged repairs.
- Fishing inventory persistence fix.
- Sub-zero rain normalization.
- Currency prose normalization and legacy display normalization.

Compatibility:
- Existing saves migrate player_status automatically.
- Existing map discovery schema remains valid.
- New locations receive generic persistent location state and familiarity/investigation slots through existing migration loops.
- No authored story gates were moved or replaced.
