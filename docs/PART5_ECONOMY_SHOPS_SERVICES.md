# Part 5 — Economy, Money, Shops and Services

Part 5 adds a bounded economy substrate connected to Part 1 locations and opening hours and Part 4 gardening.

Implemented: authoritative item/shop catalogues; mutable shop stock; purchases; seed packets feeding garden seed stock; household supply inventory; bakery food purchases; produce sales; persistent transaction ledger; earnings/spending totals; old-save migration; cumulative diagnostics through Part 5.

Deliberately deferred: regular wages and work shifts (Part 6), dynamic event price shocks (Part 7), deeper seasonal supply variation (Part 8), and supernatural economy corruption (Part 12).
