Audit findings and fixes during development:
1. New expansion locations initially lacked migrated location_state slots. Fixed with generic persistent place-state defaults for every WORLD location.
2. New visible ordinary-life actions were initially exposed by the UI action list but absent from the authoritative perform_life_activity specs/allow-list. Fixed; this was caught by visible-action fuzz testing.
3. New locations were initially absent from the investigation observation dictionaries, causing KeyError during investigation. Added ordinary and deeper observations for every expansion location.
4. New local-history discovery IDs were initially absent from the authoritative display-text mapping, causing KeyError during hobby execution. Added grounded text for all new history discoveries.
5. Historical version-lineage diagnostics only recognized branches through v0.5.x. Extended them to accept v0.6.x without weakening their behavioural assertions.
