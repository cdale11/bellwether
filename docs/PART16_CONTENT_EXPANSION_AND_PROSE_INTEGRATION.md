# Part 16 — Major Content Expansion and Prose Integration

Part 16 begins the content-density phase on top of the Part 1–15 substrate. It deliberately deepens connected ordinary life before multiplying disconnected locations.

## Added vertical slices

- Cooking consumes actual Part 4 harvest and Part 5 household food state.
- Four authored recipes provide different ingredient combinations, time costs, meals and cooking skill progression.
- Cottage restoration consumes purchased repair supplies and permanently changes restoration state, cottage-care progression and home-care skill.
- Seasonal cottage prose gives all ten seasonal phases distinct ordinary sensory identity.
- All new progression is persistent and old saves migrate safely.

This Part is a first content integration pass, not final prose completion. Part 17 long blind playthroughs must identify pacing gaps, repetitive prose, content starvation, economy imbalance and unreachable arcs before release certification.
