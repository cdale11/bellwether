BELLWETHER v1.4.0 — ADAPTIVE HORROR AND FAILURE DEPTH AUDIT

Scope
- Audited the v1.3.0 backend/core, AI provider/runtime, Director paths, content catalogues, frontend surfaces, diagnostics and retained regression tools before modification.
- Preserved the accepted systems and public game architecture; v1.4.0 extends rather than replaces the established horror, danger, recovery, recurrence, society, economy and life-simulation layers.

Findings addressed
1. The diagnostic controller could spend most of its finite budget on one semantically stalled target.
2. Cooking/community/procedural goals were labels, not prerequisite stages.
3. The diagnostic world disabled async procedural proposals, making natural procedural coverage impossible by construction even though controlled lifecycle tests passed.
4. Failure/recovery had authored hazards and recovery routes but lacked a unified inspectable adaptive vulnerability profile.
5. Society generational certification was only seven days long.
6. Diagnostic reporting needed goal-control, procedural pipeline and behavioural telemetry.

v1.4.0 changes
- Added prerequisite-stage projection for cooking, community, procedural content, social contact and gardening.
- Reduced semantic action clutter passed to the AI player and penalised goal-adjacent but irrelevant domestic/passive actions.
- Added bounded per-goal attempts, semantic stall detection, deferral and later retry in the exhaustive tester.
- Applied the already-certified procedural specialist choice to the isolated diagnostic world so public discovery can be tested honestly.
- Added adaptive failure pressure derived from poverty, injury, psychological strain, village decline and social isolation, offset by bounded mitigations.
- Added pressure bands, dominant vulnerability profile, daily history and warning telemetry.
- Added adversarial failure-pressure and preparation-mitigation certification.
- Added deterministic 90-day society soak certification.
- Expanded report dimensions with attempts per goal, maximum goal concentration, deferrals, location diversity, passive-action share, per-goal control accounting and procedural pipeline counters.
- Preserved authored-only supernatural anomalies and bounded recovery routes. LLM authority remains constrained.
