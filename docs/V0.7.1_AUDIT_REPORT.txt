Bellwether v0.7.1 — NPC Cognition and Long-Term Memory
Audit summary

Implemented:
- Persistent cognition state for all six core NPCs.
- Explicit fact/belief/rumour/impression/suspicion distinction.
- Confidence, provenance, reinforcement and revision logs.
- Concerns, unresolved questions, short-term goals and long-term ambitions.
- Authoritative bootstrap from authored obligations/personal threads and catalogue-backed knowledge.
- Witness-gated structured-event ingestion.
- Bounded cognition retrieval added to NPC dialogue context.
- Daily fading of weak subjective beliefs; authoritative facts are retained.
- Safe old-save migration.
- README rebuilt for the actual current release and direct Ollama workflow.

Important authority invariants checked:
- Cognition cannot mutate weather or story flags.
- Invalid belief kinds are rejected.
- Non-witnesses cannot ingest structured events.
- Generated conversation prose is not promoted to objective truth.
- Town Mind remains strategic context and does not directly write NPC beliefs.

Audit findings fixed during development:
1. Cognition bootstrap was initially capable of reinforcing catalogue beliefs every time migration/view refresh ran. A persistent bootstrap_complete guard was added per NPC, making initialization idempotent.
2. README.md was stale and internally contradictory, beginning with v0.6.1 while describing v0.5.2. It was replaced with a current v0.7.1 README.
3. Cognition was kept separate from the older knowledge catalogue rather than replacing it. Catalogue knowledge remains bounded information state; cognition provides subjective interpretation and goals.

Known limitations:
- Cognition updates are currently mostly deterministic and event-driven. A later specialist cognition Director may propose bounded revisions, but should not be added until benchmarked and validated.
- Forgetting is intentionally conservative and day-based. High-salience subjective memories do not yet use a richer consolidation schedule.
- Lightweight background residents remain on the cheaper population simulation tier and do not receive full core-NPC cognition state.
- Live Ollama quality/latency was not certified in this build environment. Deterministic architecture, migration, authority boundaries and routing-compatible context were tested.
