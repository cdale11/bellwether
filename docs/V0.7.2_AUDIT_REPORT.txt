Implementation safeguards
- Procedural arcs can only be selected from authored legal templates.
- LLM selection cannot invent actors, stages, facts, evidence, residents or outcomes.
- Maximum two active arcs.
- Arc proposal scheduling is infrequent for low-end hardware.
- Stage effects create structured memory events and bounded subjective concerns.
- Player involvement is only exposed at the authoritative arc location and becomes a structured witnessed event.
- Starting an arc does not mutate objective story flags, weather or money.
- Old saves migrate safely.
