# Part 8 — Seasonal Village Life and Ecology

Part 8 makes season an active ordinary-life substrate rather than header decoration. Authored seasonal profiles now describe vegetation, wildlife and village rhythm. Runtime ecology derives from authoritative season, weather and time, feeds visible ambient state, persists daily snapshots, and exposes location-aware ecology context for future activities, events, Directors and horror corruption.

This Part deliberately establishes normal ecological patterns before supernatural violations. It does not yet add supernatural ecology, full animal populations, crop pests, festivals, or season turnover across very long runs; those remain later extensions.
