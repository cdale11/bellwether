# Bellwether v2.6.0 audit — Town Consciousness Strategy

## Evidence-first findings
The v2.5.0 baseline already contained a bounded Town Mind LLM chooser, player identity inference, adaptive systemic horror, interface horror, recurrence, property, enterprise, transport and authored story authority. The Town Mind could select only broad intentions and did not persistently model what the player had built, identify leverage, or apply bounded strategic pressure. Horror adaptation selected among eligible authored anomalies but did not connect property/business/vehicle investment to a persistent adversarial strategy.

## Implemented
Town Mind schema v2 adds persistent strategic observations, dominant playstyle/leverage inference, active strategy history, pressure log, resistance score and one bounded pressure application per in-game day beginning on day 2. Strategies inspect property/cottage investment, enterprises, vehicles, relationships, routine, avoidance and inquiry. Consequences operate only on existing authoritative state and never invent canon, clues, marriages, deaths or story facts. Pressure can add small property arrears or wear, business friction, vehicle wear, psychological/social unease, routine disruption or avoidance pressure. The public view exposes an auditable town-consciousness overview.

## Boundaries
This release is a strategy substrate, not the later full story-system integration. It does not allow the LLM to mutate arbitrary state. The LLM Town Mind remains a bounded intention selector; deterministic strategic pressure is separately auditable. Authored story truth remains authoritative. Strong destructive attacks and player counterplay belong to later roadmap releases.
