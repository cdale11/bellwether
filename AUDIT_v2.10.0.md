# Bellwether v2.10.0 audit — Story, Simulation and Town Consciousness Integration

## Confirmed before editing
- Authored story progression is deterministic and owned by `story_model.py`.
- v2.9 connective scenes are deterministic and state-gated in `narrative_expansion_model.py`.
- Town Consciousness already observes player playstyle and applies bounded daily strategic pressure.
- Interface horror is presentation-only and derives from authoritative systemic-horror overlays.
- The gap was coordination: story progress, avoidance and ordinary-life investment did not produce a persistent consciousness response posture.

## Implementation boundary
Added `story_consciousness_integration_model.py`. It observes authored chapter/evidence, avoidance, ordinary-life attachment, and current Town Consciousness strategy. It chooses bounded response postures: normalize, distract, isolate, contradict, confront. It never creates evidence, facts, chapters, endings, marriages, deaths or canon.

The integration runs once per in-game day from Day 2, records auditable history, receives signals when authored connective scenes fire, and exposes a public diagnostic overview. Consequences are deliberately bounded to psychology, village focus, familiarity disruption, or supernatural pressure.

## Cleanup
Generated bytecode, Python caches, stale live reports and JSONL traces are excluded from the release package. Historical diagnostics and audits are retained as provenance/regression assets.
