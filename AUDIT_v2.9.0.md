# Bellwether v2.9.0 Audit — Authored Story & Narrative Expansion

## Evidence-first audit
The v2.8.0 story spine was inspected before editing. It already contained eight deterministic chapters, seven mystery threads, thirty evidence links, seven fact links, seven hypotheses, quest reward idempotence, six deterministic ending families, recurrence integration, and four simulation-triggered connective beats.

## Confirmed gaps
The story spine was mechanically coherent but narratively sparse: chapter advancement mostly emitted one revelation paragraph; witness depth was represented mainly by numeric trust gates; there were few authored connective scenes between simulation play and chapter transitions; and the strong distributed-truth theme was explained more often at gates than dramatized during progression.

## v2.9.0 change
Added a bounded authored narrative layer with deterministic, once-only connective scenes tied to chapter and authoritative state. It includes ordinary-life grounding, archive cross-checking, separate Mara/Jonah witness texture, boundary interpretation, ecology/social chorus framing, Eleanor's distributed-anchor method, and convergence framing. LLMs cannot author or unlock canon.

## Deliberately unchanged
No mystery solution, ending rule, quest reward, recurrence truth, or existing chapter gate was rewritten. This release deepens connective narrative without invalidating existing saves or story authority.
